Campus

The school is the best class provider of specialized security and safety system like CCTV surveillance not only at the entrance but throughout the campus

The campus-wide broadband e-connectivity with wi-fi has facilitated learning in all perspective

Computer Lab

Information Technology has become integral to every walk of life with a fully equipped computer lab.

We expose students easily to computer education. Technology brings a boost in all areas and The Hind Guru International School, Garhi Pukhta has imbibed it in all possible ways to set educational standards of a different equation. Beginning from the Pre-Primary, we focus on keyboarding, word processing, and internet skills.

Transport

The transportation needs of the students are handled by a fleet of good buses that are owned and managed by the school. Students are accompanied by a guard on the buses. The safety of the student is paramount hence adequate care is being taken for the safety of students.

Library

Children enjoy the library and the platform of information it gives. The school library fosters the development of life-long learning abilities and a love for reading in the students. Reading is important and needs to be developed in the early years of schooling.

Keeping this in mind the school has a wide range of resources in different formats in order to meet their teaching and learning needs. The collection includes books, newspapers, magazines, desktop computers, reference material.

Art & Craft

Art and craft are an expression of creativity and imagination. It is an outlet for individual thought, visualization exploration of inner creativity, expression of imagination, and interpretation of ideas, events, and environment.

The endeavor is to infuse a sense of aesthetic appreciation in each child, an ability to enjoy and appreciate beauty.
