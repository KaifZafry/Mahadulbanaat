Our Courses

The two major sources of the religion of Islam is the Quran and Hadith. These two are where the majority of the teachings come from. When looking for guidance, a Muslim often refers back to one of these two in order to educate themselves on a topic. The Quran is the central religious text of Islam.

History Of Islam

To understand the history of Islam provides the indispensable basis to understand all aspects of Islam and its culture.The history of Islam concerns the political, social, economic and cultural developments of Islamic civilisation.

Sharia and Fiqh

Fiqh expands and develops Shariah through interpretation (ijtihad) of the Quran and Sunnah by Islamic jurists (ulama) and is implemented by the rulings (fatwa) of jurists on questions presented to them.

Islam and science

Muslim scholars have developed a spectrum of viewpoints on science within the context of Islam. The Quran and Islam allows for much interpretation when it comes to science.

Islamic literature

We deals with the history of Islam, Islamic legends, and the stories of the personalities mentioned in the Quran and the Traditions of the Prophet Muhammad(Peace Be Upon Him).

Islamic calligraphy

Islamic calligraphy is the artistic practice of handwriting and calligraphy, in the languages which use Arabic alphabet or the alphabets derived from it. It includes Arabic, Persian, Ottoman, Afghan, Pakistan and Indian calligraphy.

Holy Quran Learning

Learning Quran is a very noble act, which every Muslim should be performing daily. It brings him/her near to the Allah, and will be a proof of the rewards of his/her good deeds on the Day of Judgment.
